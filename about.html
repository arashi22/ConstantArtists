CCCCCConstant is a creative management company that represents musicians, artists and producers.
Constant strives to work with the best in class, supporting artists who are driven to create what has never been created before. Then we make it our mission to tirelessly execute – and augment – their vision, whether that means partnering with the best independent labels, major studios, or tech, fashion and culture brands.
Constant Founder Ben Dickey has been cultivating long-term success for cutting-edge artists since 2004. Dickey started The Falsetto Agency in Austin, Texas in XXXX. He then traveled the world as a tour manager with bands like XXXX and XXXX. In XXXX, Dickey founded Constant Artists and merged with XXXX to create Ground Control Touring. In 2010, he opened the LA branch of Constant and The Billions Corporation in the historic Wiltern Theater building.Since 2004, Constant Artists has worked with Explosions in the Sky, Spoon, Future Islands, Tenacious D, Mates of State, Washed Out, Julian Casablancas, Wolf Parade, Karen O, Foxygen, and many others. We’ve also been fortunate to serve as a home for other managers who have continued to flourish, with Diplo, RZA, Jack Antonoff, Major Lazer, and Paul Banks.
